OficinasYA operated across multiple Madrid locations with data scattered across spreadsheets, a CRM, and a property management system. Decisions were made on gut feel because consolidating the data took hours. The dashboard changed that to seconds.

## Data Pipeline

Raw data from three sources (financial records in Excel, lead tracking in a CRM export, spatial data from the property system) was cleaned and normalized using Power Query and Python scripts. A star-schema data model connects occupancy, revenue, and pipeline into a single queryable surface.

## Key Views

- **Executive overview**: occupancy rate, MRR, lead conversion, and net margin in one glance
- **Location drill-down**: per-property performance with trend lines and target tracking
- **Lead pipeline**: funnel visualization with time-to-close and source attribution
- **Forecasting**: 90-day projection model built on seasonal patterns and confirmed contracts

## Outcome

The client moved from weekly reporting meetings (where half the time was spent correcting data) to a live dashboard that refreshes on demand. Decision velocity improved measurably.

## Impact

The dashboard gave the OficinasYA team their **first unified view**
of financial, spatial, and lead-management data — three streams
that had previously been read separately. The result was a clearer
understanding of how the business actually performed, delivered as
a Power BI surface they could query themselves.

The relationship became long-running: I returned in **summer 2024
as CEO Assistant**, where I led strategic and operational
initiatives that contributed to **expansion across 11 new cities
in 30 days**. The same client is now **Simplia's first live
deployment** — their full customer-facing inbound now runs through
the AI agents we co-founded Simplia to build.
